Lasagna

Description

A mouthwatering lasagna entree features layers of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles sandwiched between rich and savory marinara sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous filling of seasoned ground beef and Italian sausage. Each layer is generously sprinkled with a harmonious blend of mozzarella, Parmesan, and ricotta cheeses, creating a delightful symphony of flavors and textures. The dish is then baked to golden perfection, resulting in a bubbling, cheesy crown that beckons with its irresistible aroma. Served piping hot, this delectable lasagna is a hearty and comforting Italian classic that promises to satisfy your cravings with every forkful.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat until they are fully cooked. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce to the cooked meat and simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. In a separate saucepan, prepare the béchamel sauce by melting butter, adding flour to create a roux, and then whisking in milk until it thickens. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Preheat your oven to the desired temperature (usually around 350°F or 175°C).
  6. In a large baking dish, start assembling the lasagna. Begin with a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce, béchamel sauce, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and dollops of ricotta cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers until you've used up all your ingredients, finishing with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the lasagna is fully cooked.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve your delicious homemade lasagna.
