Smash Burgers

Description

A mouthwatering classic smash burger is a culinary masterpiece that begins with perfectly seasoned, high-quality ground beef, typically sourced from a blend of chuck and brisket. The key to its irresistible allure lies in the cooking method - the beef is expertly smashed onto a sizzling hot griddle, resulting in a caramelized, crispy exterior that locks in all the juicy goodness. Nestled between a soft, toasted bun, this burger features a symphony of flavors, including melty American cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato slices, pickles, and a slathering of creamy, tangy special sauce. With every bite, you'll experience a delightful balance of textures and tastes, making this classic smash burger an icon of indulgence and satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (blend of chuck and brisket)
  • American cheese slices
  • Fresh hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Special sauce (mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, and pickle relish)
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle or flat-top skillet over high heat until it's very hot.
  2. Divide the ground beef into equal portions and form them into balls.
  3. Place a ball of ground beef onto the hot griddle.
  4. Use a spatula to smash the beef down firmly, creating a thin patty.
  5. Season the patty with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook the patty for about 2-3 minutes until it develops a crispy crust.
  7. Flip the patty and immediately add a slice of American cheese on top.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the cheese is melted and the patty is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  9. While the patty is cooking, toast the hamburger buns on the griddle until they are lightly browned.
  10. Assemble the burger by placing lettuce, tomato slices, and pickle slices on the bottom bun.
  11. Place the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the veggies.
  12. Spread a generous amount of special sauce on the top bun.
  13. Place the top bun on the patty to complete the burger.
  14. Serve your delicious classic smash burger immediately.
